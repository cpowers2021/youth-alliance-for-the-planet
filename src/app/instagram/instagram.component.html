Liquid Nanoclay is a game-changing development by the Norwegian company, Desert Control, that turns dry desert land into agricultural land. The Nanoclay contains just clay and water and can be sprayed on desert sand to increase the water holding ability and nutrients in the soil. In fact, each batch of Liquid Nanoclay contains the Mychorrizal Fungi to kickstart the growth of seeds. According to Desert Control, the soil will become fully farmable in only seven hours after the application of the Liquid Nanoclay. Furthermore, the soil will last for 5 years and can reduce water usage by 50-65% compared to the previous methods of agriculture. This innovation could potentially revolutionize agriculture because desert countries in the Middle East and Northern Africa can lessen their dependence on foreign countries for food and bolster their economy.
